Good Morning, Mr. Stupka,
 
Our San Fernando Cathedral sacramental records for the time period you are researching are available on two online platforms. Although the original registers are housed in our Archives, all sacramental records are the property of the Archdiocese of San Antonio.
For your convenience:

Ancestry.com (paid subscription): Sacramental records from 1703–1920

FamilySearch.org (free account required): Sacramental records from 1703–1959

We encourage you to begin your search using these resources, as they contain many of the historical records maintained by our office. 
Please note that any questions regarding the availability, quality, downloading, or printing of records on Ancestry should be directed to the respective platform. The digital images are provided through their services, and any copyright or usage policies associated with those images are governed by the platform on which they are accessed, rather than by our office.

I am writing to ask if you could kindly help with any of the following:

Confirm whether the original baptismal register for December 1812 is still preserved by San Fernando Cathedral or the Archdiocese of San Antonio.

My goal is to document Juana's life as accurately as possible using primary source material and to help preserve the history of one of Texas' lesser-known but important historical figures.
